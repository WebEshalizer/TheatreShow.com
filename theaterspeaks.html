The Phantom of the Opera

Story: Ann Wuehler

Director: Charles Abomeli
Review: 'The Phantom of the Opera' continues to captivate audiences worldwide. The powerful vocals of the leads, combined with the intricate staging and stunning costumes, create an unforgettable theatrical experience.
Safar-e-Azad

Story: Fasana-e-Azad

Director: Firdous Jamal
Review: 'Safar-e-Azad' is a testament to the talent and creativity of Urdu theatre and is definitely worth watching. I had the pleasure of watching Safar-e-Azad, and it was an absolute delight. The director's vision shines through in every aspect of the production.
Les Misérables

Story: Another Day, Another Destiny

Director: Victor Hugo's
Review: Victor Hugo's epic tale of love, redemption, and revolution is brought to life in the musical masterpiece 'Les Misérables.' The powerful performances, emotionally charged music, and intricate set design transport viewers to 19th-century.
Zangoora

Story: The Gypsy Prince

Director: Hussain Kuwajerwala, Sadanand Patil
Review: Step into a world of magic, music, and mesmerizing performances with 'Zangoora: The Gypsy Prince.' 'Zangoora' offers a unique and unforgettable theatrical experience. Get ready to be swept away by the energy, spectacle, and sheer magic of 'Zangoora: The Gypsy Prince'!
